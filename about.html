About Y-Axis Internation Education Services

Who we are?

We are the best Visa and Immigration Consultancy in Nepal

We provide the right information, at the right time, to the right people to turn dreams into reality

Finding a consultancy that provides authentic and reliable information can be a tough challenge. We have been there, we know the consequences of being provided with the wrong information. Hence, we are here to bridge the gap between you and the right information to make your life a success story. Established in 2015 with a single motto to “show the power of Global Education”, today Y-Axis Nepal has presumably become Nepal’s best Immigration Consultancy. The credit goes to every single member of our team who lives up to the expectations of our customers and offers big time career counselling. We know the value and importance of quality education for overall growth of a country. Our purpose is to take the educational sphere of Nepal to the next level. We not only help students choose the right career path, but also ensure that they impact and inspire other people throughout their life.

What do we do?

From selecting the right university to applying for admission, from filing the visa to finding an accomodation, from boarding the first flight to supporting until you settle down permanently- We take care of everything.

WY-Axis Nepal takes immense interest in knowing and understanding the true feelings and core goals that a customer wants to achieve. Only after that, we give the information that ultimately brings out the actual vision and mission of our clients.

We follow a practical approach while dealing with the client. Here is a roadmap that shows the various steps we take to reach the final destination-

  • 1. Profiling Stage - Customer profile analysis
  • 2. Deep Analysis Stage - Perusing the Interest level towards the respective course/university
  • 3. Research stage - Finding out the facts, data, future prospects of the course/ university, potential job and career opportunities
  • 4. Evaluation stage - Shortlisting the information collected
  • 5. Review stage - Illustrating the information & data
  • 6. Delivery stage - Handing over the relevant information to customer
  • 7. Execution Stage​ - Creating a full-fledged future plan
  • 8. Guidance and support throughout the admission process and post-admission queries

Our Story
The ideation of Y-Axis Nepal dates back to 2009, when our Managing Director, Nilamber Kumar Singh, was just any young boy, who aspired to become a Sim Card Engineer. He went to Scotland to pursue a Higher National Certificate Program. Later, he joined Lovely Professional University to do B.Tech Information Technology and MBA. But life was not as smooth as he imagined it to be. Lack of authentic information and misleading guidance from consultancies in Nepal always became an obstacle in his path to success. Hence, he decided to open a consultancy that thinks beyond the boundary of profit so that no other aspiring individual has to suffer in future like he did. Y-Axis Nepal leaves no stone unturned in finding the best options for its clients. We strongly believe that nothing is impossible if you have the strong will to do it. We empathize and aim to inspire, persuade, motivate, counsel and guide the customers in the direction of ultimate success. We never compromise in carrying out extensive research to collect information and insights. What makes our clients trust us is the transparency and ethics we follow in the entire process. We offer end-to-end support and guidance from the point you enter our doorstep to the point when you permanently settle down in a country.
