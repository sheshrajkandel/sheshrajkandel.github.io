FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Are admissions open at Lovely Professional University for 2020-21?

Yes, the admission process has started at Lovely Professional University. During the current lockdown period due to COVID-19 pandemic, to support candidates, LPU has initiated a fully online application to admission process. Visit the link https://www.lpu.in/admissions-2020/ or call 18001034431 for all the detailed information and admission process.

What steps Lovely Professional University has taken to support new students in taking admission?
Lovely Professional University is closely monitoring the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and has taken multiple initiatives to support prospective candidates to pursue their higher studies in best possible way:
  • Provisional admission can be taken by paying a nominal fee of Rs. 10,000 only.
  • Flexibility to pay balance fees till 30th June 2020 to help students during these pressing times.
  • Cutoff reduced from 80% to 70% in qualifying exam to support deserving students in obtaining higher education.
  • Scholarship will also be offered on the basis of qualification previous to Eligibility qualification. For example, someone seeking admission in B.Tech can seek scholarship on the basis of 10+2 (Eligibility Qualification) or 10th (Qualification previous to 10+2)
  • New 'Covid-19 Frontline Scholarship' scheme for the wards of Doctors, Nurses, Media personnel, Police personnel, Bank employees, District Administration, Sanitation workers, Other Health care professionals and many more.
  • Liberal refund policy, deduction of Rs. 1000 only from the programme fees till 31st July 2020 and balance is refunded.
Since there is complete lockdown in the country, how can I complete the admission process?
Admission process is fully online and applicants are not required to visit personally anywhere. Visit https://admission.lpu.in/ to initiate your online admission process. Even the entrance test and Interviews are also being conducted through remotely proctored online mode depending upon requirement as per programme eligibility. The candidate can appear for the test and interview from their homes only.
What are the various courses/programmes offered at LPU?
LPU offered more than 200 programmes in 40+ disciplines. You can visit university website www.lpu.in for details about various Programmes
